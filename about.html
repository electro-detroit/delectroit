About Delectroit

An interactive map documenting sound-art venues, installations, and related places across Detroit.

Overview

Delectroit is a small mapping project that highlights locations connected to Detroit's sound-art community. Use the map to explore venues and public installations, click markers for details, and use the Menu to zoom or fit all markers.
